    Rules

    • All pawns start in home.
    • A pawn can leave home only on a 6.
    • If no pawn can move and the roll is not 6, the turn ends.
    • Rolling 6 gives another dice throw.
    • On a 6, move a pawn in play or bring one out of home.
    • All selected colors are controlled manually.
    • Capturing on a non-X square gives one extra dice throw.
    • Landing on an enemy sends it back to base.
    • Shield squares are safe from capture.
    • S squares draw UNO or Snake & Ladder cards.
    • All 4 tokens must reach the center to win.
